# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## 1. Project Overview
**Project Name:** DropZone Chauffeur
**Description:** A premium, private chauffeur application designed for airport transfers and business transport within the United Arab Emirates (UAE).

## 2. Target Audience
- High-end business travelers and corporate clients.
- Airport arrivals/departures requiring reliable and luxurious transport.

## 3. Key Business Objectives
- Provide a seamless and modern booking experience for chauffeur services.
- Ensure reliable payment processing, including international 3D Secure (3DS) compliance.
- Offer real-time trip tracking with high accuracy.
- Support multi-language capabilities (English and Arabic RTL) to cater to the UAE demographic.

## 4. Feature Requirements
- **Authentication:** Secure login using Firebase Auth.
- **Booking Flow:** Intuitive scheduling and booking of rides.
- **Live Trip Tracking:** Real-time driver location tracking via Google Maps, with accurate ETA updates.
- **Payments:** Secure online payments via Stripe, including webhook handling and 3DS challenge flows.
- **Localization:** Full English and Arabic support (RTL-ready).
- **Analytics & Crashlytics:** Tracking booking events and application stability via Firebase.
