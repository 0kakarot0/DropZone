- Require status checks: CI (analyze + test)
- Require linear history (optional)
- Restrict force pushes

## develop
- Require PRs before merging
- Require status checks: CI (analyze + test)

## release/*
- Require PRs before merging
- Require status checks: CI (analyze + test)

## Additional
